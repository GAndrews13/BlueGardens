FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

How much does postage cost?

We offer postage as part of the cost of the product, this means not only is it simple and honest as to what you'll pay for each product, but we can also offer the best value delivery for each order.

What is porousware?

Porousware is a chemical resin we apply to products. This means that you are able to extend the life and state of your products at a minimal cost, this means your gnomes and garden furniture can last undamaged for longer.

What about your suppliers? Are they any good?

We at NBGardens accept only the highest quality products, this means that we source only the best suppliers and options for the highest quality products and services.

What is your Satisfaction Guarantee Promise?

We want you to be happy with your product, this means that we offer a service to make sure you are happy with your product, not just the promise of the product. When your product arrives you are given 30 days to pay for your product, this means that if you are not happy with the product you can send it back and never have to worry about paying for a product that isn't good enough.

NB Gardens is a traditional catalogue company based in the North-West of England, established since 1875.

NB Gardens specialises in gnomes, gnome accessories and garden ornaments and it prides itself on the speed and quality of its service to its expanding customer base.

The company is renowned for the quality of its products, sourcing its stock from only the finest suppliers. Its main customer base can be classified as ‘loyal’ and predominantly ‘high value’.
